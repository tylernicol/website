CUT

Product Design Experiment

In collaboration with Mike Rousselle

What is it?

The objective when creating CUT was to redesign a household product to make it 'smart' but without a conventional screen. CUT is a cutting board that tracks nutritional information and records recipes in a database displayed using a projector.

My role in this assignment was to develop a user interface and experience for the "smart" aspect of the cutting board. I consulted members of our target audience and then built an interface around how they used their own cutting boards. The consensus seemed to revolve around the dilemma of unclean hands when handling cookbooks or a mobile device. CUT offers a hands free solution to step by step instructions.

Other quality of life features were pitched to increase the value of device. Features such as having Amazon Alexa built in to provide the option of ordering groceries directly from the cutting board, as well as having a social component to share recipes on social media.
