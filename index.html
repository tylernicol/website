Vertex

Vertex is a home media player built for simplicity without subtracting from the beautiful user interface. It minimizes the complications that come with proprietary software like iTunes and allows users to drag and drop movies in a manner comparable to a flash drive.

Saulter Street Brewery

Saulter Street Brewery is one of Toronto's newest craft breweries. The Website has a unique design akin to their unique image. The brewery has already been featured in many publications and has become the official craft beer of Cirque Du Soleil: VOLTA

CUT

The goal behind creating CUT was to re-design a household product and make it 'smart' without using a conventional screen. CUT is a cutting board that tracks the nutritional information of the food you are cooking and records your recipe in a database that is displayed to the user using a projector
